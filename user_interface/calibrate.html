Whiteboard Calibration

Set the four corners of your whiteboard

Step 1

Take a calibration image

Point the camera at your whiteboard so the whole board is visible, then capture. You can also upload an existing photo.

Step 2

Drag the corners to match your whiteboard

Four orange handles mark the whiteboard corners. Drag each one to exactly match the corner of your physical whiteboard. Order: Top-Left → Top-Right → Bottom-Right → Bottom-Left.

✅ Active Calibration

These corners will be used for every new capture.
